About

What's under the hood

This app is trained on the classic Student Performance dataset. Categorical fields are encoded, numerical scores are scaled, and a regression model — Linear Regression came out on top during evaluation — handles the prediction. The whole pipeline runs through a Flask backend, from form submission to model inference.
